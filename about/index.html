About Us

Kody's Attic, named after our mascot and pet rabbit, is a small local business started by a couple passionate about furniture restoration. It all started with a shared love of everything vintage. We often collected unique antique pieces for our home that we would also restore. Eventually, we ran out of space but still had a passion for antiques. So, we turned that passion into a small business where we could still find unique antique furniture pieces others could enjoy in their homes. Kody's Attic has something for everyone. Whether it is a simple coat of paint or a piece with a little more decorative pazazz, we breathe new life into tired old antiques. Each piece we source has a unique history, perfect for that conversation starter in your home. While we primarily concentrate on refinishing furniture, we occasionally restore certain pieces. This depends on the condition and age of the piece. By purchasing one of our vintage pieces, you also can do your part for the environment by keeping them from landfills.
