Refurbish. Refinish. Redesign.

Kody's Attic Specializes in breathing new life into second-hand vintage furniture at affordable prices. We primarily offer painted pieces, but we may offer restored pieces depending on furniture condition and availability. We offer restoration services upon special request during certain times of the year. In addition, we also have a section of special hand-crafted items for sale.

Secretary Desk

We stripped away the old stain, varnish, and sanded back the staining throughout the piece then refinished it with a fresh coat of stain and protective antique finish. We got fid of the modern bin pulls and replaced them with stunning age-appropriate early 1900s pulls. We cleaned up the insert and added a new decorative trim to the paper compartment. Last but not least we added a protective plastic inside the desk area to protect the piece from any staining due to drinks or ink. The result is a beautiful elegant secretary fit to be the centerpiece of any room.
